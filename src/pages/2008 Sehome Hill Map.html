    Directions

    How to get to Sehome Hill From I-5:

    1. Take the Samish Way exit (Exit 252) to Bill MacDonald Parkway.
    2. Continue up Bill McDonald Parkway until you reach the edge of Western Washington University.
    3. Turn right on the road just before Buchanan Towers (across from the Washington Archive Building at 26th).
    4. From this point, you may continue up to the top of Sehome Hill and park.

    Current projects

    Sounds Of the Arboretum

    The Sehome Hill Arboretum Sound Map is an interactive map of the Arboretum capturing the variety of sounds of the Sehome Hill. Each soundscape has a journal entry attached, describing what the author sees and hears. This is an ongoing project led by Dr. Felicia Youngblood.

    Future Improvements To The Sehome Hill Arboretum Maps

    Stay tuned for a listing of mapping projects that Faculty, Staff, and Students are working on to help ecologically and sustainably maintain the Sehome Hill Arboretum as a place for all to enjoy with safety and equity in mind.
